Welcome to the Mapping Tool

This platform provides a comprehensive interface for managing mapping projects efficiently. It includes tools for defining drive collection areas, estimating drive times, tracking progress, exporting data, and much more. Stakeholders can review interactive wireframes of the tool's functionalities, organized across core modules and role-specific screens.

Navigate through the menu on the left to explore detailed screens and wireframes that represent the design and workflow of the Mapping Tool. Each screen highlights a specific functionality to streamline mapping operations and improve productivity.
